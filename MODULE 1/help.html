🎉 Community Event Portal - Help Center

Welcome to the Help Center. Here you can find information about registering for events, managing your preferences, and solving common issues.

📌 How to Register for an Event

  • Navigate to the Register section.
  • Enter your Name, Email, and Phone Number.
  • Select your preferred Event Type.
  • Check the event fee displayed automatically.
  • Click the Submit button to complete registration.

🎭 Available Event Categories

  • 🎵 Music Events
  • 🏃 Sports Competitions
  • 🍔 Food Festivals
  • 💃 Cultural Programs
  • 🎨 Art Exhibitions
  • 📚 Book & Literature Events

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is registration free?

A: First-time registration is free for new users.

Q: Can I change my event preference later?

A: Yes, your event preference can be updated anytime.

Q: Why is my location required?

A: Location helps us suggest nearby community events.

🔧 Troubleshooting

  • Ensure your phone number contains 10 digits.
  • Enable location permissions for nearby event suggestions.
  • Refresh the page if images or videos do not load properly.
  • Clear browser cache if you experience display issues.
